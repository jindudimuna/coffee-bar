OUR STORY

Discover the true spirit of coffee where art and design meet. Visit a timeless space that features a beautifully designed interior, approachable service, and quality craftsmanship.

Coffee Bar by Premium Blend is about my sister’s love for coffee, my love for design, our partner’s architectural brilliance, and our need to share that a combination of these passions with the world.

Acting on a long term love in 2012, my sister opened, arguably, the first coffee shop in Abuja. That first shop closed after 3 years for us to relocate to a better location. That, unfortunately, took longer than planned. However, in 2017, I was asked by my sister to handle the interior design of the next shop for her, and that saw the start of my love for coffee. We created a beautiful cosy industrial space that served great coffee and even better sandwiches.

By the time 2020 rolled around, we were again keen to move to an even bigger site that would allow us to truly create a space that reflected our style and standard and to further add to the now burgeoning coffee culture in Abuja. We are very excited to reintroduce ourselves once again at our new space that embodies all that we are in craftsmanship, design, and service. Coffee Bar by Premium Blend is not just for the coffee lover, it is for the aesthete and biophile that is truly ergonomic.

Live for the moments shared at Coffee Bar either on the go or with great company and conversation. Open 7 days a week, we are driven to provide comfort to anyone who appreciates it. See for yourself how we defined relaxation through the intentional design of space.
